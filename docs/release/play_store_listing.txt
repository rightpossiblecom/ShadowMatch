App Name: ShadowMatch

Short description:
Fast puzzle game: match each shape to its correct shadow.

Full description:
ShadowMatch is a quick visual puzzle game built to train recognition and speed.

How it works:
- A shape appears on screen
- You choose the correct matching shadow from 3 options
- You get instant feedback and move to the next round

Modes:
- Classic: 3 lives, endless rounds, difficulty increases as you improve
- Time Attack: 60 seconds to score as many correct matches as possible

Designed to feel:
- Fast and responsive
- Clean and easy to read
- Satisfying when you get it right

Privacy:
ShadowMatch does not collect personal data. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics.
